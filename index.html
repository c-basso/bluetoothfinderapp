Losing your Bluetooth devices can be frustrating. Bluetooth Finder helps you quickly locate AirPods, smartwatches, headphones, and other Bluetooth-enabled gadgets nearby. Quickly scan your surroundings and track nearby Bluetooth devices using clear, real-time guidance.

Features

Scan and Detect Devices Instantly

Automatically detects all powered-on Bluetooth devices within range. No complicated setup. Just instant results.

Hot & Cold Guidance

Intuitive hot & cold system gives you feedback as you move closer. Cold (far). Warm (closer). Hot (near). Very Hot (almost there!).

Proximity Percentage Indicator

See the percentage of closeness to your device for precise feedback. The higher the percentage, the closer you are.

Detailed Device Information

Get manufacturer, model, and device name for every nearby Bluetooth device.

Works Everywhere

Perfect for lost AirPods at home, office, or on public transport. Works with any powered-on Bluetooth device.

How to Use

Open the app and scan for nearby Bluetooth devices. Choose your device from the list. Move around following the hot & cold signals and proximity percentage until you find it. Once the app indicates "very hot," your device is right near you.
